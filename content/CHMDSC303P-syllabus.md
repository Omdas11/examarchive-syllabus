Practical 
(Inorganic, Organic and Physical Chemistry) 
Contact Hours: 60; Credits: 04 
Full Marks = 100[End Semester Exam (70) Internal Assessment (30)] 
Pass Marks = 40 [End Semester Exam (28) Internal Assessment(12)] 
Examination Time: 18 hours (3 days) 
Section-A (inorganic Chemistry) 
1. Iodo-/lodimetric Titration and Gravimetric (any one) 
20 marks 
1) 
Determination of copper (II) using sodium thiosulphate solution iodimetrically 
11) Determination of available chlorine in bleaching powder iodometrically. 
iii) Determination of nickel (II) as Ni(DMG)2 complex gravimetrically. 
Section-B (Organic Chemistry) 
2. Qualitative Organic analysis 
30 marks 
1) Detection of elements (N, S and halogens) and functional groups, determination of 
melting points and preparation of suitable derivatives to identify the given organic 
compounds 
Section-C (Physical Chemistry) 
3. Any one experiment out of the following can set in examination 
20 Marks 
1) pH metric titration of mixture of strong and weak acid vs strong base. 
ii) To determine the water of crystallization of FeSO4.2H2O by titration against standard 
KMn0Oa.. 
11) Conductometric titration of strong acid vs strong base. 
iv) Verification of Lambert-Beer’s law and determine the concentration of 
CuSO4/KMnOq4/K2Cr207 in a solution of unknown concentration. 
v) Study of the kinetics of interaction of crystal violet/phenolphthalein with sodium
