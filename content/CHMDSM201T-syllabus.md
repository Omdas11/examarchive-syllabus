Concept of standard state and standard enthalpies of formations, integral and differential enthalpies of solution and dilution. Calculation of bond energy, bond dissociation energy and resonance energy from thermochemical data. Variation of enthalpy of a reaction with temperature— Kirchhoff’s equation. | 12 | chemistry |

Vapour pressure-composition and temperature composition curves of ideal and non-ideal solutions. Distillation of solutions. Azeotropes. Phases, components and degrees of freedom of a system, criteria of phase equilibrium. Gibbs Phase Rule. Phase diagrams of one-component systems (water and sulphur). | 12 | chemistry |

Reactions: cis -additio n (alk. KMnO 4) and trans - addition(bromine), Addition of HX (Markownikoff’s and anti Markownikoff’s addition), Hydration, Ozonolysis.  Alkynes:  Preparation: Acetylene from CaC2 and conversion into higher alkynes; by dehalogenation oftetra halides and dehydr ohalogenation of vicinal dihalides. Reactions: formation of metal acetylides, addition of bromine and alkaline KMnO 4, ozonolysis and oxidation with hot alk. KMnO 4. | 12 | chemistry |

Aryl Halides: Preparation: (Chloro, bromo and iodo -benze ne) from phenol, Sandmeyer& Gattermann reactions.
