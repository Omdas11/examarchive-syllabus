Course Objective 
The main objective of this course is 
1. To introduce the concept of limits, continuity, differentiability of functions 
and their various applications. 
2. To learn the techniques of LHospital rule for evaluation of limit. 
3. To explain the concept of definite integral and various types of reduction 
formula for integration of trigonometric function. 
4. To explain the applications in finding the area and rectification of plane 
curves; the volume and surface area of revolution of curve.
