## Syllabus

| unit_number | syllabus_content | lectures | tags |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Wave functions, Observables and Operator:** Review of Basic concepts of Postulates of Quantum Mechanics, Concept of probability and probability current density, Dirac delta function and its properties, Dirac bra-ket formalism, Adjoint of an operator, Eigenvalue problem, degeneracy, orthogonality of eigenfunctions, Hermitian operator, Commutator algebra, coordinate and momentum representation, uncertainty principle. | 11 | quantum-mechanics,wave-function,operators,dirac-notation |
| 2 | **Applications of Schrodinger's Equation:** Time dependent and time independent Schrödinger equation, Application of Schrodinger's equation to one dimensional problem: Free particle, particle in a box, potential step, rectangular potential barrier, penetration of a barrier, Square well potential, Linear harmonic oscillator (power series method). | 13 | schrodinger-equation,applications,harmonic-oscillator |
| 3 | **Matrix Representations of operators:** Matrix representation of states and operators, Continuous and discrete basis system, Unitary transformation. **Operator method to Harmonic Oscillator:** Creation and annihilation operators, properties of these operators, Harmonic oscillator problem by operator method. **Hydrogen atom:** Solution of the radial equation, energy eigenvalues, Stationary state wavefunctions. | 14 | matrix-representation,harmonic-oscillator,hydrogen-atom |
| 4 | **Conservation laws and Angular Momentum Algebra:** Orbital angular momentum (L), Commutation relations for L, Spin angular momentum (S), Total angular momentum (J) Commutation relation for J, Eigenvalues and Eigen functions of L^2, L_z, J^2 and J_z, Addition of angular momenta, Clebsch-Gordon coefficients. | 10 | angular-momentum,conservation-laws,spin |
| 5 | **Time-Independent Perturbation Theory:** Non-degenerate and degenerate perturbation theory, First and second order perturbation, Application to anharmonic oscillator, First order Stark effect in hydrogen atom, Zeeman effect in hydrogen atom, variation method and its application to ground state of helium atom, WKB approximation, WKB method for one dimensional bound states. | 14 | perturbation-theory,stark-effect,zeeman-effect,wkb |
