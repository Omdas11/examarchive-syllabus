## Syllabus

| unit_number | syllabus_content | lectures | tags |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Time Dependent Perturbation Theory:** Time dependent perturbation theory, transition probability, constant and harmonic perturbation, Fermi's golden rule, selection rules for electric dipole transitions, sudden and adiabatic approximations. | 12 | perturbation-theory,fermis-golden-rule,transitions |
| 2 | **Scattering Theory:** Scattering amplitude, differential and total scattering cross section, Laboratory and centre of mass frames, partial wave analysis, phase shifts, optical theorem, Born approximation and its validity, applications to Coulomb and Yukawa potentials. | 12 | scattering-theory,cross-section,born-approximation |
| 3 | **Identical particles:** Indistinguishability of identical particles, symmetric and antisymmetric wave functions, Pauli's exclusion principle, connection between spin and statistics, Slater determinant, exchange energy, ortho and para helium. | 12 | identical-particles,pauli-exclusion,slater-determinant |
| 4 | **Relativistic Quantum Mechanics:** Klein-Gordon equation, its success and limitations, Dirac equation for free particle, properties of Dirac matrices, covariance of Dirac equation, positive and negative energy solutions, Dirac hole theory, spin of Dirac particle, non-relativistic limit of Dirac equation, magnetic moment of electron. | 13 | relativistic-quantum,klein-gordon,dirac-equation |
| 5 | **Many electron atoms:** Central field approximation, Thomas-Fermi model, Hartree-Fock method (qualitative idea), L-S and J-J coupling schemes, Hund's rules, equivalent and non-equivalent electrons, spectra of alkali atoms. | 11 | many-electron-atoms,hartree-fock,coupling-schemes,hunds-rules |
