## Syllabus

| unit_number | syllabus_content | lectures | tags |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Introduction to Nano Science:** Nanoscale, classification of nanomaterials (0D, 1D, 2D), size-dependent properties of materials, surface-to-volume ratio, quantum confinement effect, density of states for different dimensional systems, exciton, quantum dots, carbon nanotubes, fullerenes, graphene. | 12 | nano-science,nanomaterials,quantum-confinement |
| 2 | **Synthesis of Nanomaterials:** Top-down and bottom-up approaches, mechanical milling, laser ablation, sputtering, chemical vapor deposition, sol-gel process, hydrothermal synthesis, coprecipitation method, synthesis of metal, semiconductor and oxide nanoparticles. | 12 | synthesis,nanomaterials,sol-gel,cvd |
| 3 | **Characterization Techniques:** Principle, working and applications of X-ray diffraction (XRD), Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM), Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM), Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM), Scanning Tunneling Microscopy (STM), UV-Vis spectroscopy, Photoluminescence spectroscopy. | 12 | characterization,xrd,sem,tem,afm |
| 4 | **Nanomaterials Properties:** Optical, electrical, magnetic and mechanical properties of nanomaterials, superparamagnetism, plasmon resonance in metallic nanoparticles, size dependent band gap of semiconductor nanoparticles, applications of nanomaterials in electronics, medicine, energy and environment. | 12 | nanomaterials-properties,superparamagnetism,plasmon-resonance |
| 5 | **Material Science:** Phase diagrams, solid solutions, Hume-Rothery rules, iron-carbon phase diagram, heat treatment of steel, diffusion in solids, Fick's laws, defects in crystals (point, line, surface and volume defects), dislocations, burgers vector, creep, fatigue, fracture. Composite materials. | 12 | material-science,phase-diagrams,defects |
