## Syllabus

| unit_number | syllabus_content | lectures | tags |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Atomic Spectra:** Review of Bohr and Sommerfeld model, Hydrogen spectrum, quantum numbers, spin-orbit interaction, fine structure of hydrogen, relativistic correction to the energy levels, Lamb shift, spectra of alkali atoms, selection rules, penetrating and non-penetrating orbits. | 12 | atomic-spectra,hydrogen-spectrum,fine-structure |
| 2 | **Many electron atoms:** Two valence electron systems, L-S and J-J coupling, singlet and triplet states, spectra of helium atom, normal and anomalous Zeeman effect, Paschen-Back effect, Stark effect, Pauli's exclusion principle, Hund's rules. | 12 | many-electron-atoms,zeeman-effect,stark-effect |
| 3 | **Molecular Spectra:** Rotational spectra of diatomic molecules, rigid and non-rigid rotator, isotopic effect, vibrational spectra of diatomic molecules, harmonic and anharmonic oscillator, vibrating rotator, breakdown of Born-Oppenheimer approximation, electronic spectra of diatomic molecules, Franck-Condon principle. | 12 | molecular-spectra,rotational-spectra,vibrational-spectra |
| 4 | **Raman Spectra:** Quantum and classical theory of Raman effect, pure rotational Raman spectra, vibrational Raman spectra, selection rules, structural determination from Raman and IR spectroscopy, mutual exclusion principle, applications of Raman spectroscopy. | 12 | raman-spectra,raman-effect,spectroscopy |
| 5 | **Lasers:** Einstein's A and B coefficients, spontaneous and stimulated emission, population inversion, optical pumping, properties of laser beams, Ruby laser, He-Ne laser, CO2 laser, Nd:YAG laser, semiconductor laser, applications of lasers in science and industry, basic principle of holography. | 12 | lasers,einsteins-coefficients,holography |
